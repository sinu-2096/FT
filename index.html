❓ How to Use This Tool

This dashboard visualises post-release telemetry for Pali FTH 2025 gharial hatchlings. Select filters to reveal animal locations, then explore the map and table below.

  1. 1 Filter Data — Use the dropdowns to select specific Animal_IDs, Enclosure_IDs, Pens, or Statuses to reveal their locations on the map. Hit Apply to refresh.
  2. 2 View Details — Click any circular marker to see the animal's telemetry data, enclosure details, and coordinates.
  3. 3 Locate on Map — Click a row in the bottom data table to fly directly to that animal's marker.
  4. 4 Track Updates — Click Field Updates to read manual logs and notes from the ground team.
